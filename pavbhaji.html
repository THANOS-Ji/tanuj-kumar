Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, capsicum), boiled and mashed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 6 pav (bread rolls)
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; sauté for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Add the boiled and mashed potatoes and mixed vegetables to the pan.
  5. Add pav bhaji masala and salt. Mix everything well and mash it together using a potato masher.
  6. Add water as needed to adjust the consistency. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. In another pan, heat the remaining butter and toast the pav on both sides until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot bhaji garnished with coriander leaves alongside the toasted pav and lemon wedges.

Enjoy your Pav Bhaji!
