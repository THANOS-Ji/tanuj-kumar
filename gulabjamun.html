Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup khoya (mawa)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup milk (or as needed)
  • Oil or ghee (for frying)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (for garnish, optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix khoya, all-purpose flour, and baking soda.
  2. Add milk gradually and knead to form a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls.
  4. In a pan, heat oil or ghee for frying.
  5. Fry the balls on low heat until golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  6. In another pan, combine sugar and water to make the syrup. Add cardamom powder.
  7. Once the syrup is slightly thickened, add the fried balls and let them soak for at least 30 minutes.
  8. Garnish with chopped nuts if desired before serving.

Enjoy your Gulab Jamun!
