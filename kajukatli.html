Kaju Katli Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cashew nuts
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Ghee (for greasing)
  • Silver leaf (optional, for decoration)

Instructions

  1. Soak the cashew nuts in water for 2 hours.
  2. Drain the cashews and grind them into a fine paste using minimal water.
  3. In a pan, combine sugar and water. Cook until the sugar dissolves completely.
  4. Add the cashew paste and cardamom powder to the sugar syrup.
  5. Cook on low heat, stirring continuously until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  6. Grease a plate with ghee and pour the mixture onto it. Flatten it with a spatula.
  7. Once cooled, cut into diamond shapes.
  8. If desired, decorate with silver leaf before serving.

Enjoy your Kaju Katli!
